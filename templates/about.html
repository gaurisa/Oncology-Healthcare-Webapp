Usually the primary Procedure for the Diagnosis of a Suspected tumor is an MRI Scan of the brain.

Brain tumors can happen in the brain tissue. Brain tumors also can happen near the brain tissue. Nearby locations include nerves, the pituitary gland, the pineal gland, and the membranes that cover the surface of the brain.
